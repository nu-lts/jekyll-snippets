--- layout: post title: For Dog Lovers categories: - Read, Listen, Watch tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '18' --- If you like dogs or any animal really, you'll enjoy Street Dogs by Traer Scott.  She's a photographer who documented stray dogs in Mexico and Puerto Rico.  There are some astonishing photographs and the stories will just break your heart.  You can see some of her shots at her web site.
