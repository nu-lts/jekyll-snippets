--- layout: post title: The Future of food categories: - Information and Society - Read, Listen, Watch tags: - Environmental contamination - Food science - GMO status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- The Future of food is a DVD that we have in the library's collection. It is a documentary about GMO (Genetically Modified Organism) crops such as Canola, Corn and Soy. This film gives the average viewer a basic understanding of how food crops are genetically modified and how GM crops may effect consumers. The primary focus is on plant agriculture in the United States. I think the film maker would be a great guest speaker at one of your author talks.
