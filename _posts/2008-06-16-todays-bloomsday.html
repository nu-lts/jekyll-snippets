--- layout: post title: Today's Bloomsday! categories: - Read, Listen, Watch tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '15' --- June 16, the day Joyce's "Ulysses" takes place, is the time to have a Guiness, re-read your favorite passages (also here) or, if you're near a radio or computer tonight, listen to WBAI in New York which is broadcasting some passages read aloud, along with period music. In the Boston area, there's a festival on the Boston College Campus, and another at Back Pages Books in Waltham. The NU Libraries (PR 6019 .O9 on the Snell Third Floor) have several copies, along with essays, readers companions, adaptations, and so on nearby.
