--- layout: post title: Forgotten books categories: - Information and Society - Read, Listen, Watch tags: - books on-line - free - Full Text status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '14' --- Forgotten Books is an on-line library of books that you can read on-line for free. They are hosted by Google. The titles are listed alphabetically. The collection has 1736 books on-line. However, almost half are unreadable due to some hosting error. But in any case this is an interesting example of what books on-line look like.
