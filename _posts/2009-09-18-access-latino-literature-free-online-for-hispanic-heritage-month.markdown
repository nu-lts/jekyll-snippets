In celebration of [Hispanic Heritage Month](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Hispanic_Heritage_Month), September 15 through October 15, Alexander Street Press is offering free access to select full-text, online collections for libraries. Explore poetry, short stories, folk tales, novels, memoirs, non-fiction, and plays in both Spanish and English from Latino writers around the world. Use the links below to browse titles; when prompted, enter username: **eviews** and password: **hispanicheritage** .



	
  * [Latino Literature](http://cts.vresp.com/c/?AlexanderStreetPress/8727703eba/accddf933b/b41dc0d12d)

	
  * [Caribbean Literature](http://cts.vresp.com/c/?AlexanderStreetPress/8727703eba/accddf933b/e686a56a45)

	
  * [Latin American Women Writers](http://cts.vresp.com/c/?AlexanderStreetPress/8727703eba/accddf933b/dde2f9fc6a)


Enjoy, until mid-October!
