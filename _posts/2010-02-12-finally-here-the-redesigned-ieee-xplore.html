The redesigned IEEE Xplore categories: - Engineering - Information and Society - Library News and Events - Research Online - Serendipity tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' --- A newly redesigned interface for IEEE Xplore is scheduled to arrive on Saturday, February 13. Major new features for the new Xplore are a brand new interface, a more robust search engine, improved search and more personalization.  E-mail alerts, RSS Feeds, the ability to set your preferences, save searches and much more.  Check out the new type ahead features and the search suggestions as well. There is further information about the new interface here including searching tip sheets. Also, check out the video!
