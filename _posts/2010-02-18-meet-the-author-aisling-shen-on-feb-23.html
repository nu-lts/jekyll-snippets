--- layout: post title: 'Meet the Author: Aisling Shen on Feb 23' categories: - Read, Listen, Watch tags: - Meet the Author - Meet the Author Series status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '14' --- On Tuesday, February 23, author Aisling Shen will be talking and giving a book signing at Northeastern University for her book, A Tiger's Heart: A Memoir of A Modern Chinese Woman.  Her book is available at Snell Library. In A Tiger's Heart, Shen takes the reader on her personal journey through China's history.  From her early days in the rice fields through China's major economic boom, her story demonstrates the massive changes, both social and economic, that have shaped China today.  Her talk will be held on Friday, February 23 @ 6 pm, in 405 Ell Hall.
