--- layout: post title: Looking for tax forms? categories: - Information and Society tags: - tax forms status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' --- It's that time of year again, when we all hope that April showers will bring May tax refunds. If you're looking for Massachusetts state tax forms, we have a supply of paper forms on a table next to the Hub on the first floor of Snell Library. For federal forms, connect to the IRS web site. If you need to file in another state, forms for all 50 states can be found at the web site of the Federation of Tax Administrators. Residents of eastern Massachusetts may be eligible to extend the deadline to file to May 11 . The extension is intended to ease the burden on those among us who are still coping with the effects of flooding. (Read more here.)
