--- layout: post title: Summer Reading exhibit needs your suggestions! categories: - Read, Listen, Watch - Serendipity - Staff Interests tags: - Leisure Reading - suggested reading - Summer Reading status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '16' --- To all blog visitors/posters: We are in the process of creating a summer reading exhibit to go in the large display case just off the lobby of Snell Library. Please leave comments here with any suggestions for books that you would recommend as summer reads. For the sake of popular wisdom (I may be saying this more as a reminder to myself than anybody else), please suggest books that are fast-paced, accessible, maybe cinematic reads. We will include several new releases, but also older books that stand the test of summer-reading time. We want to include mostly books that are on the shelves in Snell. Look forward to reading your suggestions.
