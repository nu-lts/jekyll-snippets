--- layout: post title: '24 Hour Service: Full Library' categories: - Serendipity tags: [] status: draft type: post published: false meta: _edit_last: '50' --- Beginning on July 5th, the library will commence an important new change. We will be open 24 hours a day on all floors, not just  the lobby. This is an important change because it gives students additional time to study and work with additional books and other materials they may not have access to otherwise. We understand that, to many students, the library is simply a quieter and less distracting atmosphere than their dorm room and apartment. However, there are a few conditions that come with this new 24 hour access: The first condition is that you must be a student and must have a valid student ID.
