--- layout: post title: 5 Ways to Maximize Your Creativity categories: - Library News and Events - Serendipity tags: - creativity - Libraries - Meet the Author - Northeastern - Psychology - shelley carson - Snell Library - your creative brain status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '16' --- Last week, creativity and brain expert Dr. Shelley Carson visited Snell Library. She shared these five tips for maximizing your creativity. 1. Continue to actively learn and gather information throughout your life. 2. Turn off the automatic 'censor' in your brain (e.g. "that won't work because..."). 3. Carry around a notepad or recorder-- breakthroughs often happen in the strangest of places, when your censors are turned down. 4. Read about and hang out with other creative people 5. Don't be afraid of failure. Creative people fail. Dr. Carson's book, Your Creative Brain, is available for checkout at Snell Library. Thank you, Dr. Carson, for encouraging us all to be engaged explorers of the Universe.
