--- layout: post title: Snell Library now hiring student employees categories: - Serendipity tags: [] status: pending type: post published: false meta: _edit_last: '100' --- Snell Library has some great student employment positions available for the Fall in various departments like Marketing & Advancement, Digital Media Design Studio, Circulation, and Resource Sharing. The Library is looking to hire work-study eligible students and part-time students for the fall semester or for the 2011-2012 school year. Working in Snell Library is both convenient and fun! The Library is located in the heart of Northeastern’s campus, which is the perfect place to work during the busy school season. Snell Library welcomes and values student employment, and  students love working here too! Top 5 Reasons to work in Snell Library:
  1. You get first word of programming and events coming up in the Library
  2. You have inside knowledge of who and where to go to for research assistance
  3. We have ‘first Thursdays’ every month giving faculty an hour to relax, socialize and eat free donuts!
  4. No need to travel somewhere to find a study space when your shift is over because you’re already in the Library!
  5. Feeling hungry while working? We have vending machines and the only ArgoTea in Boston!
As the Marketing and Event Planning Co-Op in Snell, I enjoy working in the Library because the staff are so friendly. Everyone is quick to help whenever I need it! You also cannot beat the 5 minute walk from on-campus housing..especially when winter comes! William Bratches, the NU Archivist Co-op said he loves working in Snell because "[he] love[s] being surrounded by so much information and so many stories in the archive." With so many departments to choose from, you’re sure to find a job that suites your interests. To apply, log on to myneu and click on the student employment center. Search "part-time" or “work study” and browse through all of the available positions in Snell Library.
