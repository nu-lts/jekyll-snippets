--- layout: post title: Meet the Author of "Dogma" categories: - Library News and Events - Read, Listen, Watch tags: [] status: draft type: post published: false meta: _edit_last: '49' --- Tomorrow, Wednesday, February 29th, Lars Iyer is coming to Northeastern to talk about his new book, Dogma, as a part of Snell Library's Meet the Author series. Iyer, a senior lecturer in philosophy at Newcastle University in the United Kingdom, will be presenting Dogma, the sequel to his 2010 hit Spurious. The new novel continues the adventures of British intellectuals Lars and W., who embark on a drunken lecture tour through the American South. Proving to be another colossal mistake, the duo turn to Dogma, a semi-religious code of their own making based on their philosophical studies. Part Nietzsche, part Monty Python, part Huckleberry Finn, Dogma is a novel as ridiculous and profound as religion itself. The presentation will start at 12pm noon tomorrow in room 90 of Snell Library. Refreshments will be served, and a book signing by Iyer will follow the event. Don't miss it!
