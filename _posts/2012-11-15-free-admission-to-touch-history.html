--- layout: post title: Free Admission to Touch History categories: - Archives and Special Collections - Cinema Studies - English and American Literature - History - Music tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '49' --- The Annual International Antiquarian Book Fair is coming to Boston this weekend, November 16th-18th, at the Hynes Convention Center.  The 3-day event offers visitors an exciting opportunity to view, handle, and purchase rare books, manuscripts, and collectibles, including a signed photograph by John F. Kennedy, a first edition of The History of Mr. Polly by H.G. Wells (1910), and Miles Davis’ autographed musical manuscript.  The event will also include seminars and panel discussions. On Sunday, November 18th, all students with a student ID will have free admission.  Be sure to bring your books, free appraisals will be available Sunday from 1-3PM. For more information, please visit the Book Fair website: http://bostonbookfair.com/
